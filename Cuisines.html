Bengal Food

Bengal Cuisine is appreciated for its fabulous use of panchphoron, a term used to refer to the five essential spices, namely mustard, fenugreek seed, cumin seed, aniseed, and black cumin seed. the speciality of bengal food lies in the perfect blend of sweet and spicy flavours

Gujarati Food

The traditional Gujarati food is primarily vegetarian and has a high nutritional value. the typical Gujarati thali consists of varied kinds of lip smacking dishes. Gujarati cuisine has so much to offer and each dish hasan absolutely different cooking style

Mughal Food

Mughal cuisines is one of the most popular cuisines, whose origin can be traced back to the Mughal Empire. Mughal cuisine consists of dishes that were prepared in the kitchens Royal Emprerors. Indian cuisine is predominantly influenced by the practise of cooking in the mughal era.

South Indian Cuisine

The South Indian Cuisine is known for its light, low calorie appetizing dishes. The traditional food of South India is mainly rice based. The cuisine is famous for its wondeful mix of rice and lentils to prepare delicious lip smacking dosas, vadas, idlis and uttapams.
